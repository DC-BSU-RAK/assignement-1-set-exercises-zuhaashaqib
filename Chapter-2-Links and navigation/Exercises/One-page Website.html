Overview

*The Last of Us*, developed by Naughty Dog and released in 2013 for PlayStation 3, is set in a post-apocalyptic world twenty years after a fungal infection caused by the Cordyceps fungus has ravaged the human population, turning most infected humans into aggressive, zombie-like creatures.

Key Info

  • Release Date: June 14, 2013 (PS3); later remastered for PlayStation 4 and a remake released for PlayStation 5.
  • Genre: Action-adventure, survival horror
  • Platform: Initially PlayStation 3, later remastered for PlayStation 4, and a remake for PlayStation 5.
  • Developer: Naughty Dog
  • Publisher: Sony Computer Entertainment
  • Director: Neil Druckmann
  • Writer: Neil Druckmann
  • Composer: Gustavo Santaolalla

Creative Team

  • Neil Druckmann: Director and writer, who is one of the most prominent figures in shaping the narrative and direction of the game.

  • Bruce Straley: Game Director, worked alongside Neil Druckmann and contributed significantly to the gameplay and design.

  • Gustavo Santaolalla: Composer, whose evocative score helped set the emotional tone of the game. His music is often noted as a standout feature of the game.

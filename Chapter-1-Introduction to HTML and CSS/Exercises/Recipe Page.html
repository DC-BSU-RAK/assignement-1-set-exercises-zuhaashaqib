🍓 STRAWBERRY CHEESECAKE 🍓

A simple, no-fuss recipe for the best, most life-changing cheesecake you will ever eat: rich, creamy, and insanely delicious.

⏱ 15 min 🛎 8 servings


🍓 INGREDIENTS 🍓

  • Tea Biscuits
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk 397g
  • Cream Cheese (low fat)
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Lemon Juice
  • Gelatin Powder
  • Strawberries
    • Sugar

🍓 METHOD 🍓

  1. Combine biscuit crumbs, butter and warm water.
  2. Rub the ingredients using fingertips.
  3. Press the biscuit mixture over the base of a 20cm loose bottom cake pan.
  4. Beat cream cheese, sweetened condesed milk and vanilla in an electrical beater until mixture becomes smooth.
  5. Add the lemon juice to the cheese mixture and mix on low speed to combine all.
  6. Sprinkle gelatin over hot water, mix well to dissolve and add it to the cream cheese mixture stirring constantly.
  7. Pour cream cheese mixture into prepared base. Place in the fridge for 3 hours or until cheese mixture becomes firm.
  8. Add strawberries and sugar together and simmer over medium heat for 8-10 minutes or until texture resembles a chunky jam.
  9. Set aside and cool in a refrigerator before pouring over cheesecake.

ENJOY!
